Payment Methods

Please settle your registration fee via the following payment methods:

1) Payment by Online Visa/Master Card/ CUP (China UnionPay) (Only HK$ will be accepted)

Please be reminded to complete the online registration form as well. 

On the online payment system, please select
Event Host Department: RISUD- Research Institute for Sustainable Urban Development
Event List: D7U631W001 - Conference Registration Fee for 2nd International Conference on Sustainable Urbanization

Please make sure you select a correct event in the Event Payment Menu.

The system will send a payment acknowledgement email to you if you have provided a correct email address. If you do not receive the payment acknowledgement email, please let us know by sending an email to icsu.2015@polyu.edu.hk

Subject to our receipt of your payment, the acknowledgement email is regarded as our official receipt. No separate printed copy of official receipt will be sent. You are advised to keep the payment acknowledgement email for your record.

After submitting your payment, please send the following information by email to icsu.2015@polyu.edu.hk for verification.
