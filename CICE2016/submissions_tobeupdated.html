Submissions



Two options are available for the submission of written materials:

(1) submit an abstract followed by a full paper; and (We no longer accept full paper submission.)

(2) submit an abstract only.

Abstracts (no more than 2 pages) should be submitted to online paper submissions platform.

Deadline for submission of abstract: 15 October 2014 (extended deadline)
